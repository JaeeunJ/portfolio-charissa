CTRL + y

CTRL + y is a designathon created by a group of Waterloo students who came together to challenge aspiring designers to design for the future. It was dubbed CTRL + y to represent the popular keyboard shortcut for 'redo.' It symbolizes how we want to take the resources of the past to design the future.
My role for this project was to create a website design reflective of CTRL + y's branding and values.
