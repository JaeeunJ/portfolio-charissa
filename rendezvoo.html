UX Designs

RENDEZVOO

This project was for the 2022 McMaster Designathon.
The team designed a tool that allows organizations to easily plan and manage events while taking into account employees' schedules. My roles for this project were UI/UX Design, Prototyping, User Testing, and Branding.

Date

November 04 - 06, 2022

Tools

Figma, FigJam

The Team

3 Product Designers

The Prototype

SEE FIGMA PROTOTYPE

Problem

The lack of a method to easily plan and manage events while considering all employees.

Many organizations that follow a fully remote work model are beginning to value face-to-face time to build stronger work relationships. Unfortunately, the process of planning get-togethers is both challenging and time consuming.

There are many pain points, including a lack of contact due to the pandemic caused a shift in company culture. It is also diffcult to accomodate all employees. Finally, it is tedious to plan trips due to the many considerations.

Solution

Create a mobile application for employers to manage trips and employees to respond with ease.

We identified some initial requirements to be:

  1. Seperate Interfaces for Employers and Employees.

  2. Let Employers see Employees' Availabilities.

  3. Let Employees Respond and See Their Upcoming Events.

01/03

Stakeholder Map

Based on these results, we asked our target demographic user-research questions such as..

  1. What are the biggest differences you’ve noticed in company culture since covid?
  2. Have you met up with your co-workers or employees since the start of the COVID-19 lockdown? If so, how often?
  3. How long does it typically take to plan a trip for your team?
  4. What are some blockers or complications in the trip planning process?
